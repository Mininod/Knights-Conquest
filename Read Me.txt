Controls
-------------------------------------------------
WASD - To move

Arrow Keys - To attack in the different directions

Esc - To quit the game



Aim of the game
--------------------------------------------------
The goal of the game is to reach the trap door. This is done by finding key to unlock it hidden with in the crates.
Crates are be attacked to break them open and find the items within. Kill the slimes before they kill you stopping 
you get lower down in the sky dungeon.
